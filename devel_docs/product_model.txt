Generic produc model:

1. product name

2. product picture (can be a generic mask with a bg color, in that case the picture comes from a picture category)

3. Price (can be on sale, handled by the sale module)

4. short description (255 character description)

5. long description

6. Producer (link to producer, product on producer's website)

7. product feature (feature values in a different db table, and also feature categories)
    a. type
        FIXED:
        - numeric
        - string
        - date
        VARIABLE: (these must be registered on the order, and may change the price)
        - option (dropdown) (value in another table)
        - picture bg color (dropdown) (values in another table)
    b. display name

8. Related products (id list)

9. Compatible products (id list)
